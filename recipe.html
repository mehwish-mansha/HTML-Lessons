Aglio e Olio

A classic dish that is ready in about 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces (230g) dry spaghetti
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 large cloves garlic, minced or thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Red chili flakes to taste (optional, for heat)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a rolling boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until it is al dente (tender yet firm).

  2. Prepare the sauce: While the pasta is cooking, warm the olive oil in a large skillet or pan over medium-low heat. Add the garlic and cook gently until it is fragrant and lightly golden, being careful not to burn it. If using, add the red chili flakes now.

  3. Combine the pasta and sauce: Before draining the pasta, scoop out and reserve about 1/4 cup of the starchy pasta water. Drain the spaghetti and add it directly to the skillet with the garlic and oil mixture.

  4. Finish the dish: Toss the pasta around so it is well coated with the oil. Add a splash of the reserved pasta water if needed to help the sauce emulsify and cling to the noodles.

  5. Season and serve: Stir in the fresh parsley (if using) and season with salt and black pepper to taste. Serve immediately, topped with Parmesan cheese if desired.
